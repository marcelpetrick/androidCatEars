## androidCatEars Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** YYYY-MM-DD

androidCatEars is a camera app that adds a cat-ear overlay to photos on the
device.

## Data processed by the app

- **Camera frames:** processed on-device to detect a face and position the
  overlay. Camera frames are not sent to a server.
- **Saved photos:** when the user taps capture, the composited image is saved to
  the user's device gallery via Android MediaStore.
- **Shared photos:** when the user taps share, Android's system share sheet is
  opened and the selected target app receives the saved image URI.

## Data collected by the developer

The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with the
developer. There is no analytics SDK, advertising SDK, account system, or
backend service in the app.

## Permissions

- **Camera:** required for live preview and on-device face detection.

## Data deletion

Photos are saved in the user's gallery. The user can delete them with the
device's gallery or file-management apps.
